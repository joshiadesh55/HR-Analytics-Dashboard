# HR Analytics Dashboard - Attrition & Workforce Analysis 

A professional, data-driven **Power BI Dashboard** developed to analyze employee turnover, workforce demographics, and key retention metrics. This project helps HR teams uncover actionable insights to mitigate talent loss and optimize employee satisfaction.

##  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Employees (Count):** 1.47K active and historical workforce records.
- **Total Attrition:** 237 employees have left the organization.
- **Attrition Rate:** 16.1%, serving as the baseline benchmark for retention strategies.
- **Workforce Averages:** Average Age is **37**, Average Salary is **6.50K**, and Average Tenure is **7.01 Years**.

##  Visualizations & Insights Covered
- **Demographics:** Analysis of attrition breakdown by **Education** background and **Gender** (150 Male vs. 87 Female).
- **Age Analysis:** A distribution bar chart showing that the **26-35 age bracket** experiences the highest attrition (116 departures).
- **Financial Impact:** A horizontal bar chart mapping attrition across different **Salary Slabs**, revealing high turnover in lower income brackets (Upto 5k).
- **Operational Breakdown:** Matrix and bar charts dissecting turnover by specific **Job Roles** (e.g., Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives show higher attrition).
- **Tenure Trends:** A continuous line chart tracing **Attrition By Age / Years At Company** to pinpoint the exact years where employee flight risk peaks.


##  Design & UI/UX Best Practices
- **Theme:** Styled with a clean corporate Dark Mode interface utilizing an elegant slate-blue canvas background.
- **Color Coding:** Integrated a unified palette using professional shades of muted purple, steel blue, and soft coral accents to ensure high readability and visual consistency.
- **Slicers:** Includes interactive department-level filtering (Human Resources, Sales, Research & Development) for deep-dive tracking.

##  Tech Stack & Methodology
- **Power BI Desktop:** Core platform utilized for data modeling and interactive visual design.
- **Power Query (ETL):** Employed for comprehensive data cleaning, profile transforming, and column formatting.
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Engineered custom measures to accurately compute attrition rates, employee counts, and averages dynamically.
